Last updated: June 1, 2026

Please read this disclaimer carefully before using BiteBloom. By using the app, you acknowledge and agree to the following.

1. AI-Generated Nutrition Estimates

BiteBloom uses artificial intelligence (Google Gemini) to analyze food photos and estimate nutrition values. These estimates are approximations only and are not equivalent to laboratory-tested nutritional analysis.

The accuracy of nutrition estimates depends on many factors, including:

  • Image quality and lighting conditions
  • Visibility of all food items on the plate
  • Portion size estimation (AI estimates portions based on standard plate sizes)
  • Food preparation methods that may not be visible in a photo (oil, butter, sauces)
  • Regional variations in food recipes and ingredients
  • The inherent limitations of AI image recognition technology

Actual nutritional content of your meals may differ significantly from the values shown in the app. Do not rely on these estimates for medical or clinical purposes.

2. Not Medical or Dietary Advice

BiteBloom is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. The nutrition information, meal scores, health insights, and recommendations presented in the app are for informational and educational purposes only.

The app should not be used as a substitute for:

  • Professional medical advice from a qualified healthcare provider
  • Personalized dietary guidance from a registered dietitian or nutritionist
  • Clinical nutritional assessment or therapy
  • Diagnosis or treatment of any medical condition, eating disorder, or nutritional deficiency

3. Consult a Healthcare Professional

Before making any significant changes to your diet, caloric intake, or nutritional habits based on information from BiteBloom, you should consult with a qualified healthcare professional. This is especially important if you:

  • Have a medical condition (diabetes, heart disease, kidney disease, etc.)
  • Are pregnant or breastfeeding
  • Have a history of eating disorders
  • Are taking medications that may interact with dietary changes
  • Have food allergies or intolerances
  • Are under 18 years of age

4. No Guarantee of Accuracy

  • The appropriateness of meal scores or health suggestions for your individual needs

5. Allergies and Food Safety

BiteBloom cannot detect allergens, contaminants, or food safety issues. The app analyzes visible food items in photos and cannot identify hidden ingredients that may cause allergic reactions. Never rely on BiteBloom to determine if a food is safe for consumption, particularly if you have food allergies or sensitivities.

6. Barcode Data Disclaimer

Nutrition data obtained through barcode scanning is sourced from the Open Food Facts database, an open, community-contributed database. This data may be incomplete, outdated, or inaccurate. Always verify nutrition information on the product packaging when precision is important.
